Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the app Season Palette ("the App"). Season Palette is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following types of information to provide its services:

  • Camera Access and Face Image: The App requests access to your device's camera to perform the color analysis. With your explicit permission, a single, still image of your face is captured. This image is used solely for the purpose of color analysis and is stored locally on your device (see "Data Retention").
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device to ensure optimal performance and compatibility. This may include your device model (e.g., iPhone 13, Samsung Galaxy S22), operating system version (e.g., iOS 17.2, Android 14), and a unique device identifier that is *not* tied to your personal identity and is used only for analytical purposes. We do *not* collect any personally identifiable information through the device identifier.
  • In-App Purchases: Information related to your purchases within the App is handled through Apple's StoreKit framework.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionalities, including color analysis and personalized recommendations. The face image captured for color analysis is used exclusively to determine your optimal color palette. We send this image to Google's Gemini Developer API, a third-party service, for processing. We do not use this image for any other purpose, including but not limited to, advertising, user profiling, or training other AI models.
  • To process in-app purchases and manage subscription services.
  • To improve the App's performance and user experience.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, and other information related to the App.

3. Sharing Your Information

Season Palette does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as described below:

  • Third-Party Services: We use the following third-party services:
    • Google Gemini Developer API: As described above, we share the captured face image with Google's Gemini Developer API for color analysis.
    • Apple's StoreKit: This service is used to process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions. Apple handles all payment information directly, and we do not have access to your credit card details.
    • SuperwallKit: We use SuperwallKit to manage and present in-app purchase options and subscriptions. SuperwallKit may collect anonymized data related to your interactions with purchase screens, such as which options you view or select. This data is used to improve the user experience and is not linked to your personal identity. You can review Superwall's privacy policy here: Superwall Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement measures such as:

  • Storing the face image only within the app's secure, sandboxed storage on your device.
  • Using secure connections (HTTPS) when transmitting data to third-party services like Google's Gemini API.
  • Regularly reviewing our data handling practices to ensure they align with industry best practices.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

The face image is exclusively stored locally on your device, residing within the app's dedicated and secure storage area. It is crucial to note that this image is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. Upon uninstallation of the app, the image is automatically and permanently deleted. Additionally, you retain the option to expedite the deletion process by clearing the app's data via your device's settings. It's important to understand that the retention of the image by Google is strictly governed by Google's Gemini Developer API privacy policies, which we encourage you to review for detailed information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Season Palette reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@seasonpalette.app

9. Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes Google's Gemini Developer API for color analysis. This involves sending the captured face image to Google's servers for processing. Google's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy, which you can review here: Gemini API Additional Terms of Service and the Generative AI Additional Terms of Service.

Our app utilizes SuperwallKit for managing in app purchase. You can review Superwall's privacy policy here: Superwall Privacy Policy.
